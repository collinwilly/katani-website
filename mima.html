MIMA

MIM Associates Limited is a consultancy company whose business is to provide innovative and unique solutions in the form of (technology-based) expertise to problems faced by business, government and society in general; tools and solutions that enable your organisations or business to solve issues, create value, maximise growth and improve your business performance. The company was established in 2009 and incorporated in Tanzania as a limited liability company with two Directors. The company is housed on the ground floor of Katani House on TASMA Road in Tanga City. MIM Associates has a two-member Board of Directors. The Board is the overall decision-making and policy formulation body for the company. In addition, the company has a four-member Expert Committee which acts as the technical advisory body to the company on matters of expertise and non-policy issues.

Katani Limited

Katani Limited is a Tanzanian company established in 1996 as a sisal fibre primary producer, producer of sisal products, marketer and sisal contract farming manager and producer and distributor of renewable energy.
